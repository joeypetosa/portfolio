About:

Dr. Temple Grandin was the keynote speaker in this iteration of the ISB-Town Hall Seattle Science Series. Grandin discussed her book – “The Outdoor Scientist: The Wonder of Observing the Natural World” – and a variety of other topics. ISB President Dr. Jim Heath joined at the end of her talk for a wide ranging Q&A discussion. More about the event and program here: Dr. Temple Grandin and the Importance of Getting Kids Outside.

ISB and Town Hall Seattle have put on a number of virtual events focusing on a range of important scientific issues. As Event Manager, my responsibilities included talent booking, logistical production, speaker advancement and livestream execution.
