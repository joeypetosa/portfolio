About:

A fireside chat between Providence President and CEO Dr. Rod Hochman and ISB President and Professor Dr. Jim Heath. Their hour-long discussion explored how healthcare and science will be different after COVID-19. More about the event and program here: How Will Healthcare and Scientific Research Be Different in a Post-Pandemic World?.

ISB and Town Hall Seattle have put on a number of virtual events focusing on a range of important scientific issues. As Event Manager, my responsibilities included talent booking, logistical production, speaker advancement and livestream execution.
